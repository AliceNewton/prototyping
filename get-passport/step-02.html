Next Steps

You can get the application form in 4 ways:

  • Apply online – the Identity and Passport Service (IPS) will print out the completed form and send it to you to sign and return with supporting documents
  • Collect it from a Post Office branch that uses the Passport Check & Send service
  • Request it online and IPS will send you one in the post
  • Request it by calling the Passport Advice line on 0300 222 0000

Keep a note of the barcode on the form so you can track your application
