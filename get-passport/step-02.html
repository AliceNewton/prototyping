The quickest and easiest way to renew your passport is by completing the application form online

Apply online

What you need to know

  • You can renew your passport by completing the application form. It will cost £77.50 to renew a standard 32-page passport (£90.50 for a 48-page jumbo passport).
  • You should renew if there are 9 months of less before your passport expires. Unused time up to 9 months will be added to your new passport.
  • Only book travel when your passport is valid – some countries ask for 6 months' validity on your passport.

For urgent applications

If you need your passport in less than 2 weeks, you can use the following services

  • 1-week Fast Track service, this costs £112.50
  • 1-day Premium service, this costs £129.50

Other ways to apply

Apply by phone

Call the Passport Advice line on 0300 222 0000

By post or at a Post Office

Collect it from any Post Office branch that uses the Passport Check & Send service.
