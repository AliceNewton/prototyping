The existing adult passport must be renewed, and a new child passport applied for

You should renew if there are 9 months of less before your passport expires. Unused time up to 9 months will be added to your new passport.

Only book travel when your passport is valid – some countries ask for 6 months' validity on your passport.

If you need your passport in less than 2 weeks

For urgent applications you can either use:

  • 1-week Fast Track service – this costs £112.50
  • 1-day Premium service – this costs £129.50

You can find out more about using these services in the quick answer.

You can get the application form in 4 ways:

  • Apply online – the Identity and Passport Service (IPS) will print out the completed form and send it to you to sign and return with supporting documents
  • Collect it from a Post Office branch that uses the Passport Check & Send service
  • Request it online and IPS will send you one in the post
  • Request it by calling the Passport Advice line on 0300 222 0000

Keep a note of the barcode on the form so you can track your application
