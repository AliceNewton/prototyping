Many activities and businesses require licences for legal reasons.

You can use this tool to find out which licences you may be obliged to hold in order to carry them out.

High demand licences

  1. Temporary event notice

    If you wish to hold a one-off event, you must give a temporary event notice to your local licensing authority.

  2. Registration of a food business establishment

    This is required by most types of food business, including those that store, prepare, distribute or sell food on premises, and also catering businesses run from home and mobile or temporary premises, such as stalls and vans.

  3. Application to place a skip on a public road or highway

    To put a builder's skip on a public road, you must have permission from the local authority.
